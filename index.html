Freedom and remembrance.

Finite and infinite.

Alph@bet is a literal series of NFTs that connects the past and the distant with the present and the near; connecting all times and all places to the here and now where we are in reality.
Alph@bets itself is finite, mapped to each person is infinite. And provides the richest space for imagination. Memory and imagination are not separate from each other, both are committed to going deeper into each other, and words are the key to the union between these two

"Alph@bet" is not 26 isolated/independent letters, where you can freely choose and combine code names (pronouns) that are memorable to you. It can be your family friend, your car, your pet's name, a book that has meaning to you, your nft (BAYC), your punk (cryptopunks)
Letters are just a tool to activate the mind. Their uniqueness is to find a way to feel intuitive and express (emotional) energy for everything in the world.
The vision is to build a type art community, to bring more attention to a niche and obscure profession, to support independent artists, to Perhaps the energy of individual letters may be limited, but the power of language (words) united by letters is infinite, and I hope that the community united by individual "we" can also do amazing things together.
And finally, bring diversity to the NFT community!

Hi, I'm kzizib and for the past 6 years I have been doing visual design and typography for brands. I've always been obsessed with the Fibonacci sequence and the grid system, which are the core tools of my creativity. I also have a wide range of interests and love solving puzzles.
I love all kinds of fonts from the traceable Sans-serif to the mysterious Egyptian symbols, which have always been magical to me. My teacher participated in the Morisawa Type Design Competition and won a gold medal (which always inspires me). I am forever learning.
Until the emergence of non-homogenized tokens, I knew something was going to change. As a designer I deeply believe that NFT is an effective way to provide real world benefits in a non-traditional way.
