Freedom and remembrance.

Finite and infinite.

Alph@bet is a font series of NFTs that connects the past and the distant with the present and the near; connecting all times and all places to the here and now where we are in reality.
Alph@bets itself is finite, mapped to each individual is infinite. And provides the richest space for imagination.
Memory and imagination are not separate from each other, both are committed to penetrating deeply into the other, and words are the key to combining the two.

"Alph@bet" is not 26 isolated letters, where you can freely choose and combine nicknames that are memorable for you. It can be your family's friend's, your car's, your pet's name, a book that is meaningful to you, your nft (BAYC), your punks
Letters are just a tool used to activate the mind. Their uniqueness lies in finding a way to feel the intuition of everything in the world and to express emotional energy.
The vision is to build a type art community, as well as bring more attention to equally niche and obscure professions, and support independent artists.
The power of individual letters may be limited, but the power of language united by letters is unlimited. A community of individual "I's" can do amazing things as well.
And finally, bring diversity to the NFT community!

Hi, I'm kzizib and I've been doing visual design and typography for brands for the past 6 years. Always obsessed with Fibonacci series and grid systems, which are the core tools of my creativity. Also have a wide range of interests and love solving puzzles.
I love fonts of all kinds, from traceable sans serifs to mysterious Egyptian symbols, which have always been magical to me. I entered the Morisawa Type Design Competition with my teacher and won a gold medal. I am forever learning.
With the advent of Non-Fungible Token, I knew something was about to change. As a designer, I deeply believe that NFT is an effective way to provide real-world benefits in a non-traditional way.
